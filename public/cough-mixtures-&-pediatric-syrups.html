    Cough Relief & Pediatric Care Tips

    Rest & Hydration

    Ensure plenty of rest and increase fluid intake to help thin mucus and soothe the throat.

    Humidify Air

    Use a humidifier in bedrooms to keep air moist, helping to ease coughing and congestion.

    Honey for Cough

    For children over 1 year, a teaspoon of honey before bed can help soothe nighttime coughing.

    Pediatric Dosing

    Always use the measuring device that comes with pediatric medicines for accurate dosing.

    Pediatric Age Guide

    Infants (0-1 year)

    Consult pediatrician before giving any cough medicine

    Toddlers (1-4 years)

    Use pediatric-specific formulas with doctor's approval

    Children (4-12 years)

    Age-appropriate dosing with measuring devices

    Teens (12+ years)

    May use adult formulations with appropriate dosing
