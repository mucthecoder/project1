Relief for Congestion and Cold Symptoms

At FirstChem Pharmacy, we offer a range of vapour rub products to help relieve congestion and cold symptoms. Our selection includes traditional formulas as well as specialized options like extra-strength and herbal varieties.

Vapour rubs work by releasing medicated vapors that help clear nasal passages when inhaled, providing temporary relief from congestion due to colds. They can also help soothe coughs when applied to the chest and throat area.
