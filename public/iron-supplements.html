Iron Health Benefits

Energy Boost

Iron helps transport oxygen throughout your body, combating fatigue and tiredness.

Cognitive Function

Supports brain health and improves concentration and focus.

Immune Support

Essential for a healthy immune system and fighting infections.

Pregnancy Health

Supports fetal development and prevents anemia during pregnancy.

Iron Supplement Guidance

Vitamin C Enhancement

Take with vitamin C to increase iron absorption by up to 300%.

Avoid Inhibitors

Don't take with coffee, tea or calcium supplements which reduce absorption.

Optimal Timing

Take on an empty stomach before bed for best absorption.

Medical Supervision

Always consult with a healthcare provider before starting supplements.
