    Oral Health Tips

    Brush Twice Daily

    Brush your teeth for two minutes, twice a day with fluoride toothpaste to remove plaque and prevent decay.

    Floss Regularly

    Floss daily to remove food particles and plaque between teeth where your toothbrush can't reach.

    Healthy Diet

    Limit sugary snacks and drinks which can cause tooth decay. Choose water, dairy, fruits and vegetables.

    Regular Checkups

    Visit your dentist every six months for professional cleaning and early detection of dental issues.
