Hydration & Wellness Tips

Daily Hydration

Drink at least 8 glasses (2 liters) of water daily. Increase intake during hot weather or physical activity.

Healthy Alternatives

Enhance water with fruits or herbs. Choose low-sugar beverages and natural juices for optimal health.

Electrolyte Balance

Replenish electrolytes after intense exercise with specialized sports drinks to maintain body function.

Consistent Intake

Space your fluid intake throughout the day rather than consuming large amounts at once.
